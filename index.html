the first day to my diving course

on the first day i learned only the theory. how to operate the equipment, safety procedures and navigation.

the second day to my diving course

on the second day i implemented what i learned on theory. what to do when the mask falls of your face
under water? what to do if you run out of air? all the practice done about 2-3 meters under water

the third day to my diving course

on the third day, after i pass all the test of course i went diving with my group to 30 meters underwater!
it was fantastic, almost like going to space!
